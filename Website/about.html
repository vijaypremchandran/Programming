Hi there!


I’m Nandhinie Vijay and I’m a Wedding and Portrait photographer in Youngstown, OH. I’m a natural light photographer and love how natural light can bring a glow to my images. Firstly, I’m a mother to my two boys—Athul(9) and Eshaan(5) and wife to Vijay. We have been married for 11 years and I’ve loved every second of it.

When you don’t find me with my camera or behind my computer, I’m either in my studio— spending several hours, painting or in the kitchen baking my kids favorite cakes and cookies. Speaking of baking, I think I’ve finally mastered the art of baking French Macarons with a perfect feet(hah)!

I truly believe that unconditional love and a strong family has the power to transform ones life for the better and overcome challenges that life throws at us and I feel that God has blessed me with an incredibly beautiful family who loves me for who I am. Every human is born with a purpose and I feel so lucky to serve my brides, couples, mothers and families and capture the love and sweet moments on their very special day and build lasting relationships with them. I am a people centered photographer and can’t wait to serve you with genuine love and provide you with a stress free experience from the word go!

Want to know more about me, dive right in!

I was born and raised in India. We moved to the States in 2014. I’m raising two lovely, kind and gentle boys! Athul, my older son tells me every single day, that he loves me more than I could ever love him and Eshaan my little one, whispers in my ear, “me too mommy” and it melts my heart.

I met Vijay through a common friend when I was in college and fell head over heels in love with him and married him in 2008 and I blush every single time when I talk about it!

I’m an awesome cook. I can be done cooking the most elaborate vegetarian South Indian lunch in less than 2 hours. I go crazy for a veggie burrito bowl from Chipotle, a hot plate of vegetable biryani or a good portion of Tiramasu from Olive Garden. Eating one of the above or all of the above can make me cry, yes! I am an emotional eater!(is that even a thing, whatever) And yes, this might sound weird, I have the habit of smelling the food before I eat!

We are a family of singers!Vijay and I love singing and god has blessed us with boys with mesmerizing voices! Our Friday nights include karaoke, Netflix and lots of food!
